# 🗺️ Project Roadmap — Post-Discharge Patient Care Agent

> **Status:** Backend Ready | Frontend In Progress | Mobile Planned  
> **Last Updated:** November 2025

## 🎯 Vision

Transform the validated IBM Lab prototype into a production-ready, HIPAA-compliant, multi-platform AI agent deployable in hospital systems to reduce readmission rates and improve patient outcomes.
